## Personal details

Hello, my name is Boudhayan Bhattacharya but I usually go by the alias _bbhtt_ online. I live in India. I completed my Bachelors (Honours) in Mathematics in June 2021 with 83.66% marks
and I completed  my Masters in Mathematics in June 2023 with 80.3% marks. I'm currently doing Ph.D. in Mathematical Biology. I teach part-time.

I'm interested in [Free and Open Source Software (FOSS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_and_open-source_software), technology and privacy.

I like watching movies, TV, anime; reading books; playing games and listening to music. I play online chess sometimes, although I never studied it, but I want to someday!
I also participate in various online communities related to FOSS.

This is a place for random things I find interesting.
