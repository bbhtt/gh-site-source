Hello, stranger!

My name is Boudhayan Bhattacharya but I usually go by the alias _bbhtt_ online.

I completed my Bachelors (Honours) in Mathematics in June 2021 and
Masters in Mathematics in June 2023. I'm currently doing Ph.D. in
Mathematical Biology. I teach and sometimes contribute to various [FOSS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_and_open-source_software)
projects.

I like watching movies and TV, reading books and listening to music.

I play online chess sometimes, although I never studied it seriously. I want to though, someday!

This is a place for random things in life that I find interesting.
